## How to Set Up Each Revenue Stream

### Step 1: Payment Processing
1. **Create PayPal Business Account**: paypal.com/business
2. **Create Stripe Account**: stripe.com (for subscription payments)
3. **Set up Patreon**: patreon.com (easiest for recurring donations)

### Step 2: Implement Premium Membership
**Option A: Patreon Integration**
- Create tiered membership levels ($5, $9.99, $19.99/month)
- Host premium content on Patreon
- Link from website to Patreon exclusive posts

**Option B: Gumroad + Password Protection**
- Sell monthly subscriptions on Gumroad
- Create password-protected pages on your website
- Email subscribers the password monthly

**Option C: MemberStack (Advanced)**
- Integrate MemberStack with your HTML site
- No backend coding needed
- Automatic paywall and user management

### Step 3: Join Affiliate Programs
1. **Amazon Associates**:
   - Sign up at affiliate-program.amazon.com
   - Get your affiliate ID
   - Replace `?tag=youraffiliateID` in support.html with your actual ID
   
2. **Udemy Affiliate**:
   - Apply at udemy.com/affiliate
   - Get your referral link
   - Update links in support.html

3. **Islamic Bookstores**:
   - Contact stores like Kube Publishing, Dar-us-Salam
   - Ask about affiliate programs
